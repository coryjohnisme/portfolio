AcutePlus Website

date

jun '22 - aug '22

role

ux designer + identity designer

challenge

AcutePlus is an equestrian clinic based benefit program. The AcutePlus team needs to have a way to educated and enroll members.

solution

Design an app for the accounting team that helps with collecting, submiting, and organizing financial documents.

Design a website for AcutePlus that showcases benefits, pricing, and availability of the program to future members.

responsibilities

Conduct interviews, build wireframes, lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes, conduct usability studies, and iteration.

Download Case Study

process

ideate & research

I worked with the AcutePlus team to discuss their needs. With an understanding of their needs, I was able to create empathy maps. The primary user group identified through research was multi-cultural equestrians who prioritize time over costs.

This user group confirmed initial assumptions about AcutePlus needs, and research also revealed that a device compatibility is a major concern of the primary user group.

wireframe & prototype

Taking the time to draft iterations of each screen of the website on paper ensured that the elements that made it to digital wireframes would be well-suited to address user pain points.

As the initial design phase continued, I made sure to base screen designs on feedback and findings fromÂ  the user research.

design & refine

Early designs allowed for some customization, but after the usability studies, I added streamlined descriptions to each plan. I also revised the design so information was less repetative.

The final high-fidelity prototype presented cleaner user flows for understanding and enrolling. It also reached users accessibility needs for navigating.
