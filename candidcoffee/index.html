Brand & Identity

As the design partner for Candid Coffee, I created a captivating brand identity that captures the essence of classic South Beach Miami with a Palm Beach vintage feel.

The logo and mark I designed for Candid Coffee evoke a sense of nostalgia and sophistication. By cleverly utilizing negative space within the "C," we incorporated a coffee bean, creating a visually compelling figure-ground relationship.

In addition to the logo and mark, I developed a comprehensive branding package that encompassed logo variations, color palette selection, typography treatment, and custom icons. This cohesive visual system ensures consistency and reinforces Candid Coffee's unique brand personality.

Going beyond digital design, I extended the brand identity to physical touchpoints, including coffee bag packaging, cups, and other merchandise. These tactile elements further enhance the brand experience and create a cohesive brand presence across various customer interactions.
