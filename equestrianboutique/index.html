Brand & Identity

As the design partner for Equestrian Boutique, I spearheaded the development of their captivating brand identity, defining their unique presence in the mobile equestrian fashion industry.

I employed a strong grid system to create their distinctive horse head logo, merging elegance and power to reflect the essence of Equestrian Boutique.

In addition to the logo, I incorporated a horse jump symbolizing the passion and dynamic spirit of the equestrian world.

To further establish their brand, I meticulously crafted EQ-Type, a custom typeface that embodies the elegance and sophistication synonymous with Equestrian Boutique.

Going beyond digital design, I translated the brand identity into stunning visual experiences by creating full wraps for their van and trailer, ensuring a cohesive and eye-catching presence on the go.
