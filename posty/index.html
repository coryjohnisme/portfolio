Design & Development

Posty Run, an iOS app that I conceptualized, designed, and developed from scratch, brings the vintage charm of the 1920s rubber hose animation style to life in a captivating and immersive experience.

Drawing inspiration from the aesthetics of the era, I meticulously crafted all the assets, including the characters, backgrounds, and user interface elements, to evoke the nostalgic atmosphere of the 1920s. With a keen attention to detail, I ensured that every aspect of the app reflected the unique visual style and whimsical appeal of the rubber hose animation.

Leveraging my expertise in Unity and C#, I developed the app's functionality, implementing engaging gameplay mechanics and seamless navigation. Through careful programming and animations, I brought the characters and environments to life, delivering an enchanting and interactive experience for users.
