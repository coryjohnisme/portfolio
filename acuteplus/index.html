Brand & Identity

As the design partner for AcutePlus, I created a captivating brand identity that captures their vision and sets them apart in the equine health industry.

I developed a striking horse head logo, combining elegance and strength to represent AcutePlus' commitment to excellence.

I crafted a vibrant color palette and custom icons that perfectly align with AcutePlus' energy and vitality.

I curated captivating imagery that brings AcutePlus' brand story to life, engaging equestrian enthusiasts and building trust in their cutting-edge solutions.

Additionally, I designed and developed a sleek website that integrates functionality and stunning visuals, enhancing AcutePlus' online presence.
