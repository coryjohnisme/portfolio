Brand & Identity

Serving as the design partner for Bold Rose Group, I crafted a versatile brand identity that effortlessly transitions between lighthearted and professional applications, while maintaining a timeless and highly recognizable aesthetic.

Utilizing a strong grid system, I created a simplistic and elegant rose logomark that serves as the cornerstone of the brand's visual identity.

The challenge was to develop a logo that could seamlessly adapt to both playful and corporate contexts. Through meticulous design iterations, we achieved a logo that strikes the perfect balance, meeting the client's needs in every aspect.

The resulting logo embodies the essence of Bold Rose Group, reflecting their unique personality and adaptability. Its clean and minimalistic design ensures versatility, making it suitable for a wide range of applications across various platforms.
