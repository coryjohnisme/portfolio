Brand & Identity

As the design partner for Inlet Vibes, I played a pivotal role in creating a distinctive brand identity that celebrates the essence of Northern Palm Beach County.

The logo I designed features a lighthouse, reminiscent of the iconic Jupiter Inlet Lighthouse, symbolizing strength and guiding vibes. This unique visual element instantly connects Inlet Vibes with its local roots and creates a memorable brand mark.

By carefully selecting colors and typography, I crafted a cohesive and engaging brand identity that reflects the vibrant energy of Palm Beach County. The use of local production and shirts made right here in Palm Beach County emphasizes Inlet Vibes' commitment to supporting local communities and sustainability.

Additionally, I developed an e-commerce website that not only showcases Inlet Vibes' merchandise but also offers customization capabilities. This interactive platform enhances the customer experience and allows individuals to personalize their items, further strengthening the connection between Inlet Vibes and its audience.
