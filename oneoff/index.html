Brand & Identity

One Off Exotics, a premier exotic car and lifestyle brand, entrusted me with the creation of their sophisticated and exclusive brand identity.

The logo I designed exudes power and luxury, combining a bold and thick typeface for "one" with a sleek and swift feeling "off" placed on top. The clever incorporation of a number one (#1) in the negative space of the "O" adds a subtle yet impactful touch of uniqueness and distinction.

To further elevate the brand's image, I meticulously designed and curated double-thick, linen gold letterpressed business cards. These refined and opulent business cards leave a lasting impression on anyone who receives them, reflecting the high-end nature of One Off Exotics.

In addition to the branding elements, I created a captivating landing page website that showcases the allure and allure of the exotic car lifestyle.
