Brand & Identity

Five Star Stallion, a world-renowned champion dressage horse, inspired the creation of a captivating brand identity that effortlessly captures the essence of equestrian excellence.

By cleverly transforming a horseshoe into the numeral "5" and incorporating a star icon, I instantly conveyed the prestigious and iconic nature of Five Star Stallion. This innovative logo design not only captures the equestrian aesthetic but also ensures immediate recognition and understanding of the brand.

The branding embraces the elegance and grace of dressage, reflecting the elite status and remarkable achievements of Five Star Stallion. From the refined typography to the sophisticated color palette, every element reinforces the brand's reputation as a symbol of excellence and unparalleled performance in the equestrian world.
