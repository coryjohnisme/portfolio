Brand & Identity

Bryste, a dynamic corporate and tech-forward incubator and parent company, entrusted me with the creation of their powerful and sleek brand identity.

The name "Bryste" is derived from the owner's two sons, and I ingeniously incorporated their presence in the logo design. Through the clever use of negative space in the letter "B," I created an iconic logomark featuring three overlapping figures, representing the father and his two sons. This symbolizes unity, collaboration, and the collective vision of Bryste.

The overall branding exudes a corporate and professional aesthetic, perfectly aligning with Bryste's mission. The clean lines, refined typography, and minimalistic approach evoke a sense of authority and sophistication, establishing Bryste as a formidable force in the business world.
