Logos & Marks

Introducing the revamped brand identity for Totëm, the ultimate surf shop in Saint Barthélemy! Our rebranding captures the essence of adventure with an exclusive and organic Totëm universe.

The new corporate brand identity encompasses everything from a sleek logotype to vibrant colors, captivating print media, and irresistible merchandise.

Our logo redesign retains the vintage charm cherished by skaters and surfers, while elevating it to new heights. We've carefully preserved the handcrafted feel, delivering a relaxed and custom image, all while keeping it refreshingly simple.

But that's not all! We've taken it a step further: establishing a single logo for consistency, incorporating a grid system to infuse structure into the typography while preserving the handmade aesthetic, and infusing every line with unity and organic movement.
